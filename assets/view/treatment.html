About this treatment:

  • Sildenafil is medically identical to Viagra, but available at a lower price as it is a generic medicine.
  • Sildenafil helps men with erectile dysfunction (ED) to achieve and maintain an erection.
  • Take one tablet approximately an hour before sexual activity.
  • Remains active in the body for up to four hours.

Which strength is best for me?

Choosing the correct strength

Sildenafil 25mg

This is the lowest dose, which is recommended if you found the 50mg strength effective but experienced some mild side effects.

Sildenafil 50mg

This is the recommended starting dose if you have never tried Sildenafil before.

Sildenafil 100mg

This is a high dose, which is recommended if you have tried the 50mg strength at least four times in the past without side effects but it was not as effective as desired.
